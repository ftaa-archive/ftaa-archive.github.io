Cross-Temporal Relations

Operating the FTAA's diplomatic missions, the Cross-Temporal Relations (CTR) division leverages the skills of diplomacy to create new and evolving relationships with alternate temporal eras. Currently, CTR is tasked with operating three diplomatic missions in timezones upstream of our current era. These missions have allowed the FTAA to create limited trade partnerships and information exchange programs with other eras.
