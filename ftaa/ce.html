Chronolographic Ethics

The Chronolographic Ethics division (CE) offers review services and oversight for a variety of the FTAA's operations, including temporal incursion planning and impact mitigation. CE employs a team of historians and ethicists who help maintain the highest levels of professionalism and safety among the organization's staff, all while promoting stability of the timeline.
