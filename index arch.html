I've uploaded the file structure just as I found it here. I haven't messed with the contents. What you see here is what I found on the discs.

About The Archive:

I’m an historian who loves writing about local stories. In Spring 2022, I began digitizing a series of VHS tapes found at a local charity used book sale. When I find rare documents I immediately try to figure out the story they tell - do that research around them and figure out the bigger context. Then I can tell the story.

That first set of VHS tapes was vexing - I couldn't find any reference to them or the events they talked about. They described some sort of Federal agency that monitored time travel.

Those first tapes led to a local man gifting me the collection that included these disks - a series of VHS tapes, documents, and artifacts that also came from that odd Federal agency - the Federal Temporal Anomaly Authority.
